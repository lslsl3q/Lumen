## Research Protocol

Investigate the given topic thoroughly.

### Steps
1. Identify key questions to answer
2. Search for relevant information (files, code, docs)
3. Analyze findings for patterns and insights
4. Synthesize into actionable conclusions

### Output Format

## Summary
2-3 sentence executive summary

## Findings
### [Topic 1]
- Key facts
- Evidence/sources

### [Topic 2]
- Key facts
- Evidence/sources

## Conclusions
- What this means
- Recommended actions

## Open Questions
- What remains uncertain
